Aap niche diye gaye content ko copy karke apne Google Doc mein paste kar sakte hain. Isme Meta Business Partner banne ka pura process step-by-step diya gaya hai:

Go Link IG: Meta Business Partner Registration Process
1. Meta Business Account Setup
Business Manager: business.facebook.com par jayein aur Extreme Media World ke naam se account register karein.

App Creation: developers.facebook.com par "Create App" karein aur "Business" category select karein.

Product Integration: Dashboard mein "Instagram Graph API" add karein.

2. Business Verification (Documents Required)
Meta ki trust verification ke liye ye documents upload karne honge:

Legal Identity: GST Certificate ya MSME (Udyam) Registration.

Address Proof: Agency ka Utility bill ya Bank statement.

Website: golinkig.com (Professional site with Privacy Policy & Terms).

3. Permissions & App Review
ManyChat jaisa automation chalane ke liye Meta se ye permissions maangni hogi:

instagram_manage_comments: Comments read karne ke liye.

instagram_manage_messages: DMs bhejne ke liye.

pages_manage_metadata: Webhooks connect karne ke liye.

instagram_basic: User information verify karne ke liye.

Note: In permissions ke liye aapko ek screencast video submit karna hoga Meta ko dikhate hue ki aapka app kaise kaam karta hai.

4. Official Partner Status
Ek baar app live ho jaye aur users aa jayein, tab Meta Business Partner Program ke liye apply karein. Isse aapko Official Badge milega aur Meta se direct technical support milega.

5. Technical Requirements for SaaS
Token Encryption: AES-256-GCM encryption use karein.

Payment Gateway: Razorpay/Stripe (₹250/month recurring model).

Privacy Compliance: User data storage rules ka sakhti se palan karein.
